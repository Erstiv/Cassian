The Consistency Editor reads your entire manuscript in one pass and flags anything inconsistent across chapters — character names, timelines, place details, tone drift, and violations of your world rules.

What it checks
  • 🧑 Character names, traits, and appearances stay consistent
  • 🌍 World-building details don't contradict each other
  • ⏳ Timeline events are in order and don't overlap impossibly
  • 🎭 Tone and voice don't shift unexpectedly between chapters
  • 📖 World rules (if defined) are respected throughout
