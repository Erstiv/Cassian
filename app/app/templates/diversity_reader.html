The Diversity Reader reviews each chapter for representation, cultural accuracy, stereotyping, and inclusive language. It flags concerns for your consideration — it does not rewrite or censor anything.

What it checks
  • ● Representation — depth and agency of characters from marginalized groups
  • ● Cultural accuracy — language, traditions, and practices
  • ● Stereotyping — unexamined assumptions about race, gender, disability, age
  • ● Language — potentially outdated or inappropriate terms
  • ● Power dynamics, disability portrayal, gender and sexuality
