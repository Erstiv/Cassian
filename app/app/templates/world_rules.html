How World Rules Work

World Rules are the facts, constraints, and decisions that define your project. Every agent reads them before making changes.

Characters: Names, ages, traits, relationships — anything an editor needs to get right.

Rules: Physical laws, magic systems, dietary constraints — things that must not be contradicted.

Style Decisions: POV, tense, tone — choices you've locked in that agents should respect.

Genre Defaults: Click "Load defaults" to pre-fill rules based on your project type.
